Buyback

We offer textbook buyback for students. Books must be in good condition with no water damage, excessive highlighting, binding damage or stains. For more info, contact us.


Store Credit

You may use store credit on stamped paperback fiction books. Store credit is given for gently used paperback and hardcover books that we have customer demand for. If we do not have cutomer demand for that particular book, you have the option still donate the book and contribute to our book drives.
